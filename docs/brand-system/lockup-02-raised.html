04 / Construction
Prefix (Co / Oo) = 0.4 × the brand size, weight 600, lifted to sit on the brand's cap-line; it carries the tagline (Care of / Orbit of).
Brand (TANNER / ONE) = Geist 800, written out in full, tracking −0.03em.
Mark at ≤32px drops the brand name; the prefix monogram (Co / Oo) stands alone as the icon.
One mark per surface. Lead accent reserved for active states — the lockup itself stays ink (or paper on dark).
