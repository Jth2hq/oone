# oone — Brand Voice

Single-author portfolio voice. Source: existing site copy in `oone.html` (About, Services, tutorial, log prompts, project descriptions, tooltip language). Refresh context: [`00-DIRECTION.md`](00-DIRECTION.md).

## Voice attributes

| We are | We are not |
|---|---|
| **Spare** — one good word over three | Terse to the point of cold |
| **Sensory** — edges, thresholds, light, weight, breath | Florid or romantic |
| **Lowercase-confident** — quiet authority, no shouting | Falsely humble or self-effacing |
| **Architectural** — composition, scale, attention | Trade jargon-y (no "ideation," "stakeholder," "synergies") |
| **Receptive** — observation before assertion | Passive or hedged |

Confidence: **high** — the existing copy already runs these consistently.

## Tone by context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| About / bio | Plain-spoken, slightly intimate | *"I care about edges, thresholds, and the moment a room becomes a feeling."* |
| Project descriptions | Declarative, factual, sense-led | *"Schematic design, massing studies, space planning."* |
| Service descriptions | Catalog-like, generous | *"Full-scope interior design from concept through construction documentation."* |
| Log prompts | One open question | *"what did you notice?"* |
| Tooltips / tutorial | Whispered, deferential | *"Before we begin."* |
| Buttons / CTAs | Lowercase verb, single word where possible | *log · expand · collapse cv · send* |
| Errors / empty states | Honest, no apology pile-on | *"nothing logged yet."* (vs. "Oops! It looks like…") |
| Meta / labels | Tracked uppercase, mono | *IDEAS · ARTIFACTS · SENSATIONS* |

## Vocabulary

**Use freely**
attention, threshold, edge, surface, weight, scale, room, light, breath, time, place, material, palette, condition, presence, gesture, drift, season, observe, notice, arrange, hold, return.

**Avoid**
synergy, leverage, holistic, journey (as metaphor), passion, unlock, empower, curated (overused), cutting-edge, world-class, bespoke (overused), seamless, immersive, ecosystem, solutions.

**Brand-specific terminology** (use these exact terms — they are load-bearing in the UI)
- **ideas / artifacts / sensations** — the three log categories. Lowercase always.
- **itself / orbit** — the two relationship modes for an entry. Lowercase.
- **log** — the verb and the panel. Never "journal," "notes," "entries."
- **scheme** + **variation** — the theming system. The five schemes have proper names: **FERRO · COBALT · PATINA · SUMMER · STONE**. Always uppercase, never quoted.
- **field** — the canvas (thermal heatmap). Not "background" or "canvas."
- **drawer** — the bottom-rising panel. Not "modal" or "sheet."
- **bubble** — the navigation orbs. Not "button" or "node."

## Sentence patterns

- **Sensory triplet.** Three nouns or short phrases in series, no filler. *"edges, thresholds, and the moment a room becomes a feeling."*
- **Em-dash aside.** Use sparingly to widen a thought. *"My work moves between scales — development strategy down to mosaic detailing — but the question is always the same."*
- **One open question per surface.** *"how does this shape attention?"* / *"what did you notice?"* — never two in a row.
- **Catalog cadence.** Service descriptions are noun phrases separated by commas, no verbs. *"Schematic design, massing studies, space planning."*
- **No exclamations. No emoji. No ellipses for drama.**

## Capitalization & punctuation

- UI buttons & CTAs: **lowercase** (`log`, `expand`, `send`, `see full cv`)
- Section labels (eyebrows, nav, panel headers): **TRACKED UPPERCASE** in mono
- Body copy: **sentence case** (italic Cormorant for headlines)
- Scheme names: **UPPERCASE** in body copy, no quotes (FERRO, COBALT)
- Em-dashes: spaced ` — ` (not `—` flush)
- Oxford comma: yes
- Periods on UI button labels: never. Periods in tooltip body: yes.

## Before / after

**1. Service description**
- Before: *"We offer cutting-edge architectural solutions tailored to your unique vision."*
- After: *"Schematic design, massing studies, space planning."*

**2. Empty state**
- Before: *"You haven't added any entries yet! Click the + button to get started."*
- After: *"nothing logged yet."*

**3. Tutorial step**
- Before: *"Welcome! Let's take a quick tour of the amazing features."*
- After: *"Before we begin. Choose a palette."*

**4. Project tagline**
- Before: *"A truly immersive luxury spa experience that pushes boundaries."*
- After: *"A wellness room cut into the foundation. Stone, water, low light."*

**5. Form error**
- Before: *"Oops! Email address is invalid. Please try again."*
- After: *"that doesn't read as an email."*

**6. Log prompt**
- Before: *"Add a new journal entry to capture your thoughts."*
- After: *"what did you notice?"*
